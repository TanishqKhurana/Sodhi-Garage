Reviews

The Agera RS is a track-focused variant of the Koenigsegg Agera with various components implemented from the One:1 such as the 5.0-litre twin-turbo V8 engine, which features 1160 hp (865 kW) as standard but can be upgraded to one megawatt (1,341 horsepower) as an option. Unlike the One:1, the Agera RS with the upgraded engine does not meet or overlap the power-to-weight ratio, due to a slight increase of weight.
